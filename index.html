This website uses information gathered from Strive's replays on PC to track players and assign them Glicko-2 ratings based on their performance. This system is widely used in competitive games and is especially suitable for 1v1 play. Matchups are as follows:

Rating difference Win probability

10063%

30085%
40091%
50095%
If you take on someone 300 points above you in a FT10, you can expect to take about 2 games off of them.

This has been said many times before, but the tower system that Strive uses is not particularly good. Despite player skill roughly following a normal distribution (see here) the tower is heavily weighted towards the top end, with one third of games being played in Celestial. Additionally, there appear to be many players who, despite qualifying for celestial, instead hang out in floor 10 (where the middle 30% of the population is) and maintain very high win rates as a result.

This project originally started because I wanted to get more accurate info for matchup charts, but that's still ongoing.

The data on this page should be updated every 24 hours or so. Most games played prior to Dec. 16th are missing due to mistakes using the API, but anything more recent than that should be on here.
